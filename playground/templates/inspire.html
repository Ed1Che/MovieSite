A poor white kid from Detroit struggles to rise above poverty and a broken home in 8 Mile, an obscenity-strewn Rocky for the gangsta rap generation. It stars controversial artist Eminem as Rabbit, a polite, hard worker with a gift of rhyme. He's a loyal pal.

Creed

Adonis Johnson (Michael B. Jordan) never knew his famous father, boxing champion Apollo Creed, who died before Adonis was born. However, boxing is in his blood, so he seeks out Rocky Balboa (Sylvester Stallone) and asks the retired champ to be his trainer.
